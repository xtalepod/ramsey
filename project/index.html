if your are a light house then let me be your guard tower
we can never really know the moment you’ll drown
but at least you’d know that i was watching out for you
and i was ready to swim if i knew it would have made the difference

the last time something like this happened
i wrote about the surgeon’s hand carving black rock
territorial nostalgia being broken open at your rib cage
then as now, the snowing is falling in a way that soaks everything

heaving.
drive all morning, all afternoon, and decide at the door
the choice is clear: be separated or stay, so stay.
check in each day and the choice never changes

so i stay and time changes suddenly,
what was yesterday is no longer the case for today
and i realize that, as the surface shifts from slipping into wading,
i will be here waiting for you to find your way back.

softly,
softer still, stiller still
your wordless voice may she be lulled back into speaking
on water we will walk together soon, hand in hand.
